What is Ransomeware?

Ransomeware is a name for a type of software that is loded onto a computer that will encrypt one's hard drive and hold it for ransom.

How does it get on my computer?

Normally it will get onto your computer by mascarading itself as desireable software, such as a virus protector.

How to avoid installing ransomware?

Only install programs that you know about from websites that you trust. Be wary of websites that send you to an external website for a download, or that use complicated installers that offer you other kinds of programs in addition to the program of your choice.
